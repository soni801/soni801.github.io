Commands

These are our custom commands this season:

/trigger shopping

Teleports you to the shopping district. This is a place where we build community shops, and people can buy things from others.

/trigger help

Show a help menu briefly explaining commands ingame.

/trigger ch_toggle

Toggle a HUD over your hotbar that shows you your current location, the direction you're looking in, and the ingame time.

/trigger sethome

Sets a custom home to your current location.

/trigger home

Teleport back to your home you set using /trigger sethome.
