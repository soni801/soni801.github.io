Crafting

Our custom crafting recipes and recipe changes this season are:

Ender Pearl to Cyan Dye

Allows you to craft ender pearls into cyan dye.

Netherrack to Nether Bricks

Allows you to craft netherrack into nether bricks.

Gravel to Flint

Allows you to craft gravel into flint

Flint to Gravel

Allows you to craft flint into gravel.

Magma Cream to Slime

Allows you to craft magma cream to slime

Vanilla Tweaks

Some of our custom crafting recipes are from Vanilla Tweaks. These are:

Craftable Name Tags

Allows you to craft name tags

Craftable Notch Apples

Allows you to craft Notch Apples

More Stairs

Creates 8 stairs instead of 4.

Rotten Flesh to Leather

Allows you to smelt rotten flesh into leather

Back to Blocks

Allows you to craft slabs and stairs back into blocks.

Uncraftable Ice

Allows you to break down ice.

Uncraftable Nether Wart Block

Allows you to uncraft nether wart blocks.

Universal Dyeing

Allows you to dye any dyeable block, no matter what colour it is.

Wool to String

Allows you to craft wool into string.
