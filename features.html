Features

The custom features we have this season are:

Custom Paintings

Custom paintings make it possible to express yourself in completely different ways than in vanilla minecraft. Our custom paintings are:
- World Map
- District Map
- TV
- CodeBlock Logo

Custom Texts

Some of the texts in the game, like the credits, have been updated to make them funnier to read.

Custom Commands

Custom commands make you able to do much more than you previously could. The possibilities are almost endless. For more info on the custom commmands, take a look at the commands tab.

Teams

We have multiple teams on CodeBlock. Teams play together, and it is not possible to attack playes on your own team. The colour of your ingame nametag is changed according to your team.

Vanilla Tweaks

CodeBlock uses a datapack collection called Vanilla Tweaks. These are made by the team over at https://vanillatweaks.net/. All below features are from Vanilla Tweaks.

Multiplayer Sleep

Multiplayer Sleep makes us able to skip the night when only one player is sleeping. This makes it much easier to sleep on larger servers, where all players may not be able to sleep at the same time.

Player Graves

When a player dies, a grave spawns at the death location with all the players items. Only the grave owner can regain their items by crouching on top of the grave.

Anti Enderman Grief

This makes endermen unable to pick up blocks. We do this to remove the worry about endermen destroying farms or builds.

Double Shulker Shells

Shulkers drop 2 shulker shells on death instead of just 1.

More Mob Heads

Makes all mobs in the game have a small chance to drop their head upon death.

Villager Death Messages

Displays in chat when a villager dies.

Home

You can set a custom home location using /trigger sethome, and teleport back to this location using /trigger home.
